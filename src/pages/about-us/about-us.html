Rotaract Alumni Mora

This app is brought to you by Rotaract Club of Alumni of University of Moratuwa.
Rotaract Club of Alumni of University of Moratuwa, chartered in 2013 under Rotaract District 3220, is a community based Rotaract Club sponsored by the Rotary Club of Colombo West. The club was founded with the key objective of paving the path for individuals taking leave from Rotaract Club of University of Moratuwa as they graduate from the University, to continue their engagement in the Rotaract movement. In addition to outgoing member of Rotaract Club of University of Moratuwa, the Club welcomes Alumni of University of Moratuwa who wish to commence a journey in Rotaract.
